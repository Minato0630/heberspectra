3. STUDENT / PARTICIPANT SIGNIN
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Role Select: "External College Student"

Student accounts are created dynamically via the "Register" tab.
Login Username:
  - You can sign in using either your registered Email OR your
    assigned Unique Student ID (e.g. BHC-STU-1001).
Password:
  - Use the custom password created during registration.

Important Validation Rules:
  - College Check: Signup is only allowed for OTHER colleges. 
    "Bishop Heber College" will trigger warnings and block registration.
  - Duplicate Check: The system checks for existing signups matching
    the same Email, same Register Number (ReNo), or same Name + College.
    Duplicate registrations will be rejected.
  - Event Limit: Students are limited to a maximum of 2 event
    registrations. Teammates must also belong to the same college and
    be registered in the system (verifiable by their Student IDs).
